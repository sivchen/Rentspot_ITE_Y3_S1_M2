About Us

We are a team of Year 3 Information Technology Engineering students passionate about simplifying the process of finding rental rooms in Phnom Penh. Our platform is designed to help students, especially those from provinces, quickly and easily find rooms that match their preferences.

MISSION

To become the go-to platform for students seeking affordable, comfortable, and convenient rental accommodations in Phnom Penh.

VISION

Our mission is to save students time and reduce the stress of finding a place to stay by providing a simple, user-friendly platform. We aim to empower students to focus on their studies while we handle the challenges of finding the perfect room.
